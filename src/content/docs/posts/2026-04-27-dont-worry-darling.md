---
title: "Don't Worry Darling"
description: "A poem about connection, mortality, and a shared moment on a balcony under the stars."
date: 2026-04-27
tags: [poetry, personal]
---

Don't worry  
Darling,  
I am deathless,  
As long as you are,  
Because I slipped  
Some of my vibrations  
Amongst yours.  
As did you,  
mingle some stardust  
into mine.  

That night,  
on the balcony  
How we shared  
Between cigarette breaths  
how sad  
fathers can make you  
And we agreed  
To find  
less disappointing role models  
bathed in the moon light  

I could have sworn  
your halo  
peaked out, curiously,  
For just a single moment  

I wonder  
As we stood there  
Staring cathartically into the heavens  
If the stars too, stared our direction  
Hoping for a glimpse,  
Of the same kind of guidance  

-- W.w.
